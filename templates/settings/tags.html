About Tags

Tags are automatically created when you add them to recipes. Use consistent naming to keep your tags organized:

  • Sources: Track content origin (books, articles, videos)
  • Actions: Manage workflow stages (research, process, focus)
  • Thoughts: Organize ideas and personal reflections
  • Creation: Plan creative outputs (videos, articles)
