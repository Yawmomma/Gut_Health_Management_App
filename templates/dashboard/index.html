Welcome

Gut Health Management App is a local, desktop-first tool for tracking meals, symptoms, and gut health patterns related to FODMAP sensitivity and histamine intolerance. Log foods with serving sizes, capture essential nutrition details, monitor symptoms and stress, explore a detailed food guide, and learn from built-in education—everything stays on your computer with no accounts or cloud sync.
