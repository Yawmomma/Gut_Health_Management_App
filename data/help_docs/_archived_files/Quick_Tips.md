#### Safety Features:
- All recipes show combined FODMAP ratings
- Histamine levels calculated for complete meals
- Portion size tracking for better management
- Custom notes and preparation tips

#### Creating Recipes:
Use the food database to add ingredients. The app automatically calculates total FODMAP and histamine levels for your recipe.

#### Saving Meals:
Build complete meals by combining multiple foods. Perfect for planning your daily menu and ensuring balanced nutrition.
