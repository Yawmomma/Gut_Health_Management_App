Manage your personalized recipes, saved meals, and get AI-powered recipe assistance tailored to your dietary needs.

#####  What You Can Do:
- Create and save your own recipes using foods from our database
- Build and save complete meals for quick planning
- Get AI-powered recipe suggestions based on your dietary restrictions
- Track FODMAP and histamine levels for each recipe and meal

#### Safety Features:
- All recipes show combined FODMAP ratings
- Histamine levels calculated for complete meals
- Portion size tracking for better management
- Custom notes and preparation tips

#### Creating Recipes:
Use the food database to add ingredients. The app automatically calculates total FODMAP and histamine levels for your recipe.

#### Saving Meals:
Build complete meals by combining multiple foods. Perfect for planning your daily menu and ensuring balanced nutrition.
